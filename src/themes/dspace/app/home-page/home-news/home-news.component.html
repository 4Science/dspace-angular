DSpace-GLAM 7

DSpace-GLAM is the first free and open-source extension of DSpace for Cultural Heritage ever developed.

DSpace-GLAM has the institutional repository as its core component, providing high visibility online of the collected information and objects. DSpace-GLAM broadens DSpace functionality and expands its data model while remaining aligned with its code base.

DSpace-GLAM adopts and ensures compliance with international standards and practices to facilitate interoperability, data transfer and sharing.
